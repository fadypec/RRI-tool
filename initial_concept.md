RRI (Responsible Research & Innovation) Tool Concept:

**Function:** Private upload of papers/grant applications to assess RRI implications


---

**Target Use Case:**

UKRI grant applications
Standard UKRI question: *"What are the ethical RRI implications and issues relating to the proposed work?"*


---

**AREA Framework:**

**A**nticipate
**R**eflect
**E**ngage
**A**ct


---

**Key Problems Identified:**

Academics unfamiliar with RRI framework
Academics not accustomed to thinking about their work in a security context
Need for guidance on mitigating concerns


---

**Desired Outputs:**

Identification of relevant RRI implications
Suggested ways of mitigating concerns
