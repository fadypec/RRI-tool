Prototype Screen 1 of 3

A structured lens for
dual-use reflection

This tool helps you think through the security and dual-use implications of your research. It surfaces considerations you might not have encountered and helps you articulate them in your grant application.

ⓘ

This is an independent tool developed by the Centre for Long-Term Resilience. It is not endorsed by UKRI or any other funding body. It is a reflection aid, not a compliance check, and does not replace institutional review.

Nothing you enter is stored on our servers. Your assessment results exist only in your browser session.

Draft RRI text

The proposed research involves work with biological agents, specifically [organism name], which is classified as ACDP Hazard Group [X] and appears on the Australia Group list of biological agents. We have considered the dual-use implications of this work, particularly the potential for methodological transfer to other organisms.

To mitigate these risks, the project operates under the oversight of [institutional biosafety committee]. We are conducting a formal risk assessment in accordance with the BBSRC/MRC joint policy on research misuse, and will consult with our institutional biosecurity officer prior to commencing experimental work.

We have considered alternative, lower-risk experimental approaches and will employ [specific containment / methodological safeguards] throughout the project. We are committed to responsible publication practices and will review all outputs for sensitive content prior to submission.
